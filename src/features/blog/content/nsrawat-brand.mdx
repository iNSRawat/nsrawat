## Clear Space

Maintain a clear space of 1/2 the height of the Mark on all sides of the logo, except in limited cases where the logo remains clearly recognizable and legible.

## Usage Guidelines

- **Do** use the mark or logotype on a contrasting background for visibility
- **Do** maintain proper clear space around the logo
- **Don't** stretch, distort, or rotate the logo
- **Don't** apply effects like shadows or gradients to the logo
- **Don't** change the colors unless using approved brand colors
